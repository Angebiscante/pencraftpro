Help and Support

How to Reset Your Password

  1. Go to the login page and click on "Forgot your password?" link.
  2. Enter your registered email address and click "Submit".
  3. Check your email for a password reset link and click on it.
  4. Enter your new password and confirm it.
  5. Click "Reset Password" to complete the process.

How to Update the Software

  1. Open the PenCraft Pro application.
  2. Go to the "Settings" menu.
  3. Select "Check for Updates".
  4. If an update is available, click "Download and Install".
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the update.

How to Export Your Notes

  1. Open the PenCraft Pro application.
  2. Go to the "Notes" section.
  3. Select the notes you want to export.
  4. Click on the "Export" button.
  5. Choose the format (PDF, Word, etc.) and the destination folder.
  6. Click "Export" to save your notes.
