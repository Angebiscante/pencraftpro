How To Export your Notes or Drawing

To export your Notes,

Go to your notes

Click the vert icon, or the triple dots (portrait style) below, then click the Export

It will automatically saved to your Download files, as .txt


To export your Drawing,

Go to your drawing

Click the settings icon, then click the Export

Choose between PDF, JPG, PNG

It will automatically saved to your Images
