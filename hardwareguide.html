Hardware Guide

Installing PenCraft Pro

Follow these steps to install PenCraft Pro on your device:

  • Visit PenCraft Pro official PenCraft Pro website.
  • Click the Download page
  • Choose what operating system you will to download the app
  • Open the app and create your account

For software setup instructions, visit our User Guide.

PenCraft Pro supports the following OS versions:

Check which OS versions work best with PenCraft Pro:

  • Windows 8, 10, 11
  • Linux (Ubuntu) and newer
  • macOS Catalina and newer
  • APK (Android)

Stylus Support

  • PenCraft Pro is compatible with any stylus that works on touchscreens, ensuring universal support for a wide range of devices.

Using PenCraft Pro

Learn how to use PenCraft Pro with your device:

  • Open the app and sign in to your account
  • Tap the "+" button to create a new note
  • Use the toolbar to access drawing and writing tools
  • Save your notes to the cloud for easy access

Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions:

  • App not installing: Check your internet connection and available storage
  • Stylus not working: Ensure your stylus is charged and compatible
  • Sync issues: Make sure you are signed in to your account and connected to the internet
